About ProfilePro

ProfilePro is a professional networking platform designed to help users create secure profiles, upload resumes, and showcase their skills in a modern and accessible way.

Our goal is to simplify how professionals share their experience while maintaining security, privacy, and authenticity through verified accounts.

Built as part of an academic cloud and networking project, ProfilePro demonstrates real-world use of distributed systems, authentication, and scalable web design.
